## Data Collection and Storage

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data when using the app.

### Privacy-First Approach:
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require registration or login.
- **Local Data**: All user data (tasks, wins, AI feedback) is stored only on your device.
- Firebase may process limited anonymous technical data (such as device type, OS version, and app performance data) for service operation and improvement. User inputs are only processed temporarily via Firebase for AI generation and are not stored or saved.

---

## AI Processing:
- Short user-provided text entries may be temporarily transmitted to third-party AI services (Google Gemini via Firebase) solely to generate feedback.
- These entries are not intended to contain personal information.
- We do not store this content on our servers.
- AI-generated feedback is stored locally on your device and is automatically deleted within 24 hours.
- Data is transmitted securely and processed in real time.

**Links to third-party privacy policies:**
- https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## Data Deletion:
- Since all data is stored locally, deleting the app will permanently delete all user data, including AI feedback.
- We cannot restore deleted data.

---

## Security:
- Your data remains on your device; its security depends on your device settings.
- iOS standard sandboxing and encryption protections are applied automatically.
- Data is transmitted securely using HTTPS.

---

## Children’s Privacy:
Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
